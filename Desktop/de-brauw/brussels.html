Offices / Brussels

BRUSSELS

From our Brussels office, we advise our clients on all aspects of EU law, including Competition, State Aid and Data Protection, as well as foreign direct investment regulations. We assist our clients on their dealings with the European Commission and national authorities, and have a strong track record in acting before the EU and national courts.

From the heart of the European Union, we maintain excellent contacts with policymakers and authorities, and provide our clients with a swift and an effective response to regulatory investigations. Matters typically span many different jurisdictions across the globe (specifically our mergers, cartels, litigation and data protection matters), and our teams (across different time zones) assure our clients of expert and seamless advice, wherever and whenever required.

Together with our Best Friends firms, our team consists of more than 40 Brussels-based lawyers, making it one of the largest practices in Brussels. Our competition presence in seven EU Member States (with more than 175 lawyers) makes us one of the largest European competition practices covering all major European jurisdictions. We are ranked as the leading experts in these jurisdictions by all of the relevant legal directories.
